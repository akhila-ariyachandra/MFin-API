Loan /api/loan POST Create a new loan
  • token (string)
  • loanType (String)
  • date (Date) [MM-dd-YYYY]
  • loanAmount (Number)
  • duration (Number)
  • interest (Number)
  • customerID (Number)
Loan /api/loan/:loanID GET Get the details of one loan
  • x-access-token[header] or token[query] (string)
  • loanID (Number)
Loan /api/loan GET Get the details of all loans
  • x-access-token[header] or token[query] (string)

  • loanAmount (Number)
